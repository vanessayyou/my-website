ABOUT ME


Hi, my name is Yuzhe (Vanessa) You.

I am a sophmore currently studying Computer Science at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor.


My coding skills include C++/C, Python, Javascript, HTML, CSS, R/RStudio, Lua and more.


I am currently taking various EECS courses at my university and doing research as a member of SOCR team under University of Michigan's Multidisciplinary Design Program, with my focus being Data Analytics and Machine Learning.

ME AS A RESEARCHER

My research team SOCR (Statistics Online Computational Resource) designs and disseminates educational materials, web-services, and advanced methods/tools in probability, statistics, and machine learning.

This is a Faculty Research MDP team at the University of Michigan that will develop an enhanced analysis and visualization toolbox with an emphasis on "Big Data."

My current sub-team is "Data Analytics - MIMIC-III," with our focus on interrogating the MIMIC-III database, a large collection of ~43,000 critical care patients from an ICU in Boston, MA, by utilizing R/RStudio and machine learning models to digest the vital signs, laboratory results, free-text data and waveforms available in this unique dataset and predict clinical outcomes via statistical modeling tools.
