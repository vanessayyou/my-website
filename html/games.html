MY GAMES

I started making my own games senior year in high school using my spare time.

Before that, I had been more focused on making visual arts; I always thought that programming had nothing to do with me. Then I took a small class on Mobile App Development which sparked my interest in programming, and I realized that I could combine my skills in graphic design with programming and become an independent developer.

The first game I released is a mobile app game called "Vincent's Amazing World of Scary Human Heads."

The game was released in English on Apple Store, Google Play, and Taptap. But surprisingly, it received quite a bit of attention in China.

So I decided to make a sequel relevant to Vincent targeting specifically towards Chinese audience.

A few months later, I released "Vincent: Phantom of the G4" (文森：G4人偶事件).

The second game is also quite successful; I have many different Youtubers from Mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan and other places playing my game. The game received over 500,000 views on Youtube and Bilibili.com. I have also received many fanarts from fans of Vincent.

Right now I am not spending as much time making my own games, as I am more focused on my schoolworks and research at the moment. However, I am still experimenting with different programming languages and platforms by making mini games, which is a good way for me to learn new coding languages.
